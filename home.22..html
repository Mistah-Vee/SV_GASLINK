Safety Reminder

Safe gas use matters

Every delivery should end with safe handling. Here are a few quick reminders users should always keep in mind.

  • Keep cylinders upright and in a ventilated space
  • Check for leaks safely before regular use
  • Keep gas away from open flames and unsafe heat sources
